Frequently Asked Questions

The stuff blokes ask before they sign up. Straight answers, no fluff.

I've tried everything and fallen off before. Why would this be any different?+

Most programs hand you a plan and leave you to figure it out alone — that's exactly why they fall apart. You get a dedicated coach who checks in every week and adjusts the plan the moment life gets in the way. That's why 98% of our guys are still going strong past the first 90 days.

I'm flat out. How many hours a week does this actually take?+

3 to 4 hours of training a week — not 3 to 4 hours a day. The nutrition side is built around your real life: work dinners, travel and weekends all included. This was designed for busy men who don't have spare time to burn.

Do I have to go keto, carnivore or quit carbs?+

No. No banned foods, no extreme diets, no living off chicken and rice. We build the plan around the food you actually enjoy — so you can hold onto the results long after the program ends.

I hate gyms. Can I train at home?+

Absolutely. Gym or home — we build the training around whatever equipment you've got and wherever you're most likely to actually show up. No commute required.

I travel a lot and enjoy a drink. Will that wreck it?+

It won't. The whole system is designed for real life — eating out, hotel stays and a few beers with the boys included. You'll learn how to keep moving forward without putting your life on hold.

What about Ozempic and the weight-loss jabs?+

You don't need them here. We fix the habits, training and nutrition that actually keep the weight off — so you build a body you can maintain on your own, not one you're renting from a prescription.

How quickly will I actually see results?+

Most guys feel different within 2 weeks and see visible change by week 4 to 6. Across our 3,000+ members the average is 10 to 15kg down in the first 12 weeks.

What does it cost — and do I have to pay it all upfront?+

Basecamp is $3,250 paid in full, or $250 to start and $125 per week. Most guys choose the weekly option. Either way you get the full program from day one.

As soon as you send the message. We onboard you within 24 to 48 hours, you meet your coach, and you're off.
